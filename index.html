E-Notes

⚠ Saving your notes will overite the previous notes so if you are planning on starting with new notes I encourage you to download the current ones so you dont lose them. ⚠


SAVE NOTES - Saves your notes and title so next time you open the page they will be waiting for you to continue on them.
CLEAR NOTES - Removes all of your notes(you still have to save them if you want to keep them gone)
DOWNLOAD NOTES - Downloads your notes into a txt file so that you can create new notes without losing the current ones
